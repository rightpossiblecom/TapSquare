1. INTRODUCTION

TapSquare ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

The App does NOT collect any personal information. Specifically, we do NOT collect:
- Your name, email address, or contact information
- Your location data
- Your device identifiers or IMEI number
- Your browsing history or activity logs
- Your game play data (all data remains local on your device)
- Your IP address or network information

3. LOCAL DATA STORAGE

All game data (scores, preferences, settings) is stored locally on your device using Hive, a local database. This data is not synced to any cloud service, shared with third parties, or sent to external servers.

You may reset or clear all data at any time through the app's Settings screen.

4. PERMISSIONS

The App requests no sensitive permissions beyond standard app functionality required to display the user interface and play audio.

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The App does NOT use:
- Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
- Advertising networks or ad networks
- Crash reporting services
- Remote configuration services
- Cloud storage or synchronization services

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The App is designed for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, children's privacy is fully protected.

7. DATA SECURITY

Your local data is stored securely on your device. You maintain full control and can delete the App and all associated data at any time.

8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to the App. Your continued use signifies acceptance.

9. CONTACT US

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
support@tapsquare.app

10. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:
- Knowledge of what data is collected (none)
- Access to your data (local only)
- Delete your data (via Settings > Clear All Data)
- Know that we do not sell or share your data

SUMMARY: We collect no personal data. All game data stays on your device. Your privacy is our priority.
