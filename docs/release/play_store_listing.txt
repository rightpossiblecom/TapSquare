FULL DESCRIPTION:

TapSquare is a simple, addictive arcade game designed to test your reflexes. Tap the blue squares as they appear before they disappear. The longer you last, the faster and smaller the squares become.

KEY FEATURES:
- Fast-paced reaction gameplay
- Progressive difficulty that increases with your score
- Track your personal best high score
- Smooth animations and polished interface
- Customizable sound and vibration settings
- Completely free to play, no ads
- Works offline
- Privacy-first: no data collection

HOW TO PLAY:
1. Tap "Start Game" to begin a 30-second round
2. Tap the blue squares as they appear
3. Each successful tap increases your score
4. Challenge yourself to beat your high score

GAME MODES:
- Timed Mode: Play for 30 seconds and rack up as many taps as possible
- Progressive Difficulty: Squares appear faster and shrink as you score higher

SETTINGS:
- Toggle sound effects on/off
- Toggle haptic feedback/vibration on/off
- View your high score
- Reset your high score anytime
- View app information and privacy policy

PRIVACY & SAFETY:
- No personal data collection whatsoever
- No account required
- All game data stored locally on your device
- No analytics or tracking
- Safe for all ages

PERMISSIONS:
- Internet: Used only for rating and feedback features
- Vibration: For haptic feedback (optional, can be disabled)
- Audio: For sound effects (optional, can be disabled)

TapSquare is a lightweight, instant gratification game perfect for quick gaming sessions during breaks. Challenge yourself and see how high you can score!
